Introduction

The YEARS method is developed to provide an unambiguous set of criteria for determining the necessity of a CTPA in case of a suspected Pulmonary Embolism

This method is based on a combination of clinical items and the D-dimer test result value. The clinical YEARS items determine the D-dimer threshold

In case of suspected Pulmonary Embolism, the blood D-dimer must be immediately tested. Then follow the YEARS algorithm in this app
